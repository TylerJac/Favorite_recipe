Cream Puffs

Ingredients | Directions

Cream puffs are my favorite dessert because of their light, airy pastry filled with rich, creamy custard. They are both elegant and indulgent. The contrast between the crisp choux pastry and the smooth filling makes every bite delightful.

I love how versatile cream puffs can be, allowing for various fillings and toppings, making them perfect for any occasion. and everyday dinners.
